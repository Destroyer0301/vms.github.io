DigitalMarketing

Digital marketing is defined by the
use of numerous digital tactics and channels to connect with
customers where they spend much of their time: online.
The best digital marketers have a clear picture of how each digital marketing campaign
supports their overarching goals. And depending on the goals of
their marketing strategy, marketers can support a larger
campaign through the free and paid channels at their disposal.
